APP NAME: Luggage Manager
Logo and project details: https://trello.com/b/REOwIz4M/luggagemanager-crud-app
Description: 'Luggage manager' is an app where you can track your luggages that you bring during a trip. The purpose of the app is to make sure that you wont forget anything. You can chose your destination and write down all your luggages that you're bringing. You can see all your past trips, that to make sure if you want to take a refence for your new trip.

For building this app I used Django, Python, PostgreSQL, CSS, Javascript and HTML. All the functionalities are made with Python, Django and PostgreSQL. The templates are made with HTML. The styling is made with CSS and Javascript. 

The best thing is the transition that was really fun to make!

Future developments: The app requires another model for items. The purpose is to make the common user's items to get sort automatically.
