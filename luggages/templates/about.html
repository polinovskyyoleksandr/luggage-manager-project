About the app

'Luggage manager' is an app where you can track your luggages that you bring during a trip. The purpose of the app is to make sure that you wont forget anything.

Are you tired to get messy? Are you tired of getting confused in your clothing piles that you can't sort?

This app is for you! Write down your destination and add all your luggages! Chose the luggage type, size, color and add your items that you're bringing!
